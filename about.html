About AMIS Global Exports

Exporting India’s Ayurvedic Wisdom to the World with trust, purity, and international quality standards.

Who We Are

AMIS Global Exports is a Surat, Gujarat (India) based export company specializing in high-quality Ayurvedic medicines and herbal products for global markets.

Rooted in the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda and aligned with modern international quality standards, we supply safe, effective and export-ready herbal formulations worldwide.

We collaborate with reliable manufacturers and certified facilities to ensure that every product reflects authentic Ayurveda, purity of ingredients and strict quality compliance required for international trade.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

To deliver trusted Ayurvedic healthcare solutions globally while maintaining uncompromising quality, ethical sourcing and reliable business practices.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized exporter of Ayurvedic medicines, known for reliability, transparency and long-term partnerships with international buyers and distributors.

Rising Global Demand for Ayurvedic Products

The global Ayurvedic products market has been expanding rapidly. Valued at nearly USD 3.4 billion in 2019, it is expected to reach approximately USD 14.9 billion by 2026 with strong growth.

The COVID-19 pandemic increased interest in natural healthcare, boosting demand for immunity-enhancing herbal products worldwide.

Asia-Pacific currently dominates the Ayurvedic market with more than 60% global share, while demand continues to grow in Europe, the USA and other international regions.

Why Import Ayurvedic Medicines from India?

Origin of Ayurveda

India is the birthplace of Ayurveda and home to a wide variety of medicinal plants used in traditional healing.

Largest Exporter

India is the world’s largest exporter of Ayurvedic medicines and herbal products with thousands of international shipments every year.

Government Regulation

The Ministry of AYUSH regulates and promotes Ayurvedic manufacturing and export ensuring global safety and quality standards.

Certified Manufacturing

Many Indian manufacturers follow GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) standards ensuring safe and reliable herbal medicines.
